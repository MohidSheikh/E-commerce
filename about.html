About Us

Nishat Mills Limited stands as one of the most well equipped, modern and the largest vertically integrated textiles companies in Pakistan. Due to application of vigilant and tactical management, effective policies, upkeep of discipline in operations and an effective marketing strategy the growth trend is expected to continue for years to come. The company’s production facilities include spinning, weaving, processing and power generation.
